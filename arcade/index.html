Retro Shoot-'em-up Remastered

Creator: Tyler Alesse

Year Created: 2022

It was my intention to invoke a sense of nostalgia, the feeling of entering an arcade as a kid, or getting home from school and playing games on the old family computer.

Designed with two different styles of retro games mixed together, endless gameplay and stage-based gameplay, to keep the player engaged. Drawing inspiration from a mix of different games that I enjoyed as a child, and still enjoy today.

The core of the gameplay is intended to be a Galaga-esque game, with mechanics from a few other games from the late 90s and early 2000s.
